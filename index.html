Poem of my Dogs

By Parker Madrigal-Geary

My dogs are very small, they are only weenie dogs

We got them when I was in sixth and we've had them ever since

Ones name is Berkley and the other is Raider, although we never have one in favor

Recently we got another dog named Bella, she is a huge dog at only 2 years old

she likes to dig up plants and play fetch

together they even each other out

some crazy some calm

despite that they still bond

they are my group of dogs

and i wouldnt change it
